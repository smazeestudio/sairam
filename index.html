About the COnference:

     ARICE – Advanced Research and innovations in Information and Communication Engineering is a National Level Conference Conducted by the Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering of Sri Sai Ram Institute of Technology , Chennai . Department of ECE is one of the most active and Sincere Department of the college when it comes to Academics as well as Other Extra Curricular Activities. In order to showcase the energy within us for the technology as well as the best usage for the good will of society With support of our Management and our Students interest to get exposure for a national level technical learning’s we paved our way towards the Organizing of a national level conference.

     ARICE is focused on to bring in the best of the recent trends of technology . ARICE wants to be part of the World level Technology Changes . So we welcome authors to bring in their creative ideas in all the technologies and to make them proud on being of this Technology Revolution . For our Pilot Edition we are open to the theme of Networking and Communication which stands to be our Core . Any topics related or much innovative are most welcomed . ARICE is being crafted on to excite you along the Technology Horizons and give you a fruitful Learning’s .
